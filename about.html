Our Story

Founded in 2010, MDD Food began as a small family-owned restaurant with a simple mission: to serve delicious, authentic meals made with love. What started as a humble kitchen has blossomed into Nairobi's premier dining destination, known for its innovative fusion of local flavors and international techniques.

Our founder, Chef Michael Daudi, envisioned a place where quality ingredients meet creative culinary artistry. Today, we continue this tradition, sourcing the freshest local produce while maintaining our commitment to exceptional service and unforgettable dining experiences.

At MDD Food, we take immense pride in crafting each dish with the utmost care, precision, and passion. Our talented chefs use only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients to create meals that are not just delicious, but truly memorable culinary experiences. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is prepared with attention to detail, ensuring authentic flavors, beautiful presentation, and consistent excellence.

We combine culinary artistry with heartfelt hospitality because we understand that when you choose to dine with us, you're trusting us with your valuable time and special occasions. It's a responsibility we never take lightly, and one we renew our commitment to with every guest we serve. At MDD Food, your satisfaction isn't just our goal - it's our passion.
